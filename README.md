# LinguaShield

### Where language becomes an early warning system

An AI-powered Linguistic Exploitation Early Warning System (LEEWS) that helps users identify recruitment scams, coercive communication, exploitation risks, and deceptive offers through multilingual language analysis, interactive learning, and evidence organization.

## The Problem

Many trafficking and exploitation cases begin with language rather than physical movement. Deceptive job advertisements, recruitment messages, housing offers, and travel opportunities often contain warning signs that vulnerable individuals may not recognize.

Existing tools largely focus on intervention after harm occurs. There is a need for prevention-focused tools that help people identify risks earlier.
## The Solution

LinguaShield combines AI-powered linguistic analysis, interactive awareness storytelling, and evidence organization tools to help users:

- Detect risky recruitment messages
- Learn coercion tactics through branching stories
- Organize evidence into structured timelines
- Access support resources and reporting pathways
- ## Features

### Linguistic Risk Detector
Analyzes job posts, messages, and offers for exploitation indicators.

### Interactive Awareness Story
Teaches users how coercion develops through realistic scenarios.

### Evidence Timeline Builder
Transforms messages and screenshots into organized timelines.

### Safety & Support Center
Provides access to helplines, NGOs, and support resources.
## Human Rights & Ethical Design

LinguaShield follows a prevention-first and survivor-centered approach.

- No facial recognition
- No victim identification
- No surveillance functionality
- Consent-first design
- Privacy-preserving architecture
- Automatic redaction of sensitive information
- Human oversight for risk assessments
- AI outputs are advisory, not definitive
